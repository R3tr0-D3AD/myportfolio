01 — About

WHO
I AM.

I'm Christian Groenewald — a Business Management student with a genuine passion for marketing. I'm the kind of person who gets obsessed with how brands are built, why people buy, and what makes a message actually land.

What sets me apart is my background: ten years competing in gymnastics, then coaching for nearly five more. That shaped me in ways a classroom never could — discipline, precision, the ability to perform under real pressure, and the instinct to lead others to their best.

Currently in my third year of studies, planning to push through to Honours. Every day I'm connecting the dots between athletic mindset and business strategy.

"The same focus that wins competitions builds careers. I'm just applying it to a bigger stage."
